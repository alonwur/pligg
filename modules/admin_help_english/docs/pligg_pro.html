Pligg Pro

The Pligg Pro shop is the official spot to get Pligg Templates, Modules and Services. All of the items in the shop are developed by either official Pligg developers or by community members who have contributed a large amount of time working on Pligg for free. The shop gives these developers a way to earn money for their work since Pligg is already free software. A percentage of each sale also goes toward further development of the Pligg Content Management System.
